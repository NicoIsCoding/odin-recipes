Burger Recipe

The Burger of your Dreams

A burger so light and fluffy you thought could only exist in your dreams, yet here he is ready for you to eat!

What are you waiting for? Start cooking!

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Ground Beef
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Onions
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Divide ground beef into 85g balls
  2. Take a ball and smash it onto a hot cast iron plate
  3. Season the patty
  4. Flip the patty once the edges look crispy
  5. Season other side and add cheese on top
  6. Let cheese melt and take off the patty
  7. Put patty on your burger and enjoy!
