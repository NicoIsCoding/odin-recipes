Fries Recipe

The Fries of your Dreams

Fries so good they make you wanna go obese!

What are you waiting for? Start cooking!

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Frying Oil

Steps

  1. Peel potatoes
  2. Cut potatoes into fry shape
  3. Let potatoes sit in cold water for 30 min
  4. Fry potatoes
  5. Enjoy fries!
