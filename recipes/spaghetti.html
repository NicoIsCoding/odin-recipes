Spaghetti Recipe

The Spaghetti of your Dreams

Spaghetti so good you would trade your ps5 for it, you nerd!

What are you waiting for? Start cooking!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Water
  • Onions
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Get water to a boil
  2. Boil your spaghetti
  3. Prepare sauce in pan
  4. Mix tomato sauce with other ingredients
  5. Add boiled pasta to pan
  6. Combine and enjoy!
